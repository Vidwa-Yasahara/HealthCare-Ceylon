Our Islandwide Branches

HealthCare Ceylon - Colombo (main branch)

Located in the bustling heart of Colombo, our main branch is equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and highly skilled medical professionals, offering comprehensive healthcare services. The branch is known for its specialized departments in neurology and cardiology.

HealthCare Ceylon - Kandy

Nestled in the scenic town of Kandy, this branch combines modern healthcare with a peaceful environment. Known for its top-tier pediatrics and dermatology services, the Kandy branch also offers cutting-edge orthopedic treatments.

HealthCare Ceylon - Galle

The Galle branch provides patients with access to expert surgical teams and advanced healthcare technologies. Specializing in obstetrics, ENT, and ophthalmology, this branch is well-known for its maternity care and eye surgeries.

HealthCare Ceylon - Jaffna

Our northern branch in Jaffna focuses on specialized treatments, including oncology and urology. The branch also offers psychiatric services and rheumatology care, providing comprehensive treatments for both mental and physical health issues.
