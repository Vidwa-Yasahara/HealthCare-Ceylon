Our services

General Medicine

Our general medicine department provides primary healthcare for patients of all ages, offering a broad spectrum of treatments for common and complex medical conditions. From routine checkups to chronic disease management, our experienced physicians ensure comprehensive care tailored to individual health needs.

Pediatrics

We offer dedicated pediatric care for children from infancy to adolescence. Our services cover everything from immunizations, developmental screenings, and acute illness management to long-term care for chronic conditions. We focus on holistic growth and well-being, ensuring a child-friendly environment for both the child and their family.

Maternity Care

Our maternity services provide comprehensive care for expectant mothers, from prenatal counseling and regular health checkups to delivery and postpartum support. We offer specialized birthing plans, neonatal care, and postpartum recovery programs, ensuring the health and safety of both mother and baby during this critical period.

Emergency Care

Our 24/7 emergency department is staffed with highly trained professionals ready to handle life-threatening conditions, trauma, and urgent care needs. Equipped with state-of-the-art facilities, we are prepared to respond rapidly to medical emergencies, offering immediate treatment and stabilization.


Comprehensive Healthcare at HealthCare Ceylon

At Healthcare Ceylon, we offer a wide range of healthcare services to meet the needs of our patients. From General Medicine and Surgery to specialized care in Pediatrics and Maternity, our experienced team ensures you receive the best care. We also provide on-site Pharmacy, advanced Radiology services, and 24/7 Emergency Care. Along with these, we offer many other essential medical services, making us your trusted partner in health and well-being.
