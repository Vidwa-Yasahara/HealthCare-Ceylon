Welcome to HealthCare Ceylon


At HealthCare Ceylon, we are committed to delivering exceptional medical care to our community. Being one of the most famed hospitals in South Asia, Healthcare Ceylon is equipped with state of the art facilities and staffed by a team of highly experienced professionals dedicated to your health and well being. With our highly advanced and latest technological equipments We offer a comprehensive range of services, including advanced cardiology, orthopedics, pediatrics, and much more. Our mission is to provide personalized, compassionate care that meets the unique needs of each patient. Trust us to be your partner in health and wellness.


Services that we provide

Advanced Cardiology

Our cardiology department offers the latest in heart care. We are equipped with modern technology to diagnose and treat a wide range of cardiovascular conditions. Our team of specialists works closely with patients to provide personalized care, ensuring the best possible outcomes.

  • State of the art technology
  • Experienced cardiologists
  • Comprehensive care

Orthopedic Services

Our orthopedic department specializes in treating musculoskeletal issues. From joint replacements to sports injuries, our team is dedicated to getting you back on your feet. We provide a wide range of orthopedic services, including surgical and non-surgical treatments, all tailored to your specific needs.

  • Joint replacements
  • Sports injuries
  • Rehabilitation programs

Pediatric Care

Our pediatric department offers comprehensive care for children from birth through adolescence. We are dedicated to the health and well-being of your child, providing preventive care, vaccinations, and treatment for a wide range of illnesses. We believe in a family-centered approach, working closely with parents to ensure the best care for their children.

  1. Newborn Care
  2. Nutritional Guidance
  3. Emergency Pediatric Care
